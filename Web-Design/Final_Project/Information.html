Information

Practice

Practices are Monday through Friday, from 3:00 to 5:00 PM, at Mattawan High School. Meet at the silver benches.

Meets

On race day, arrive to event location at least one hour before race time. If there is a bus, meet at the silver benches at least 15 minutes prior to bus time. You are expected to stay until end of meet, unless otherwise arranged with coaches. If you leave the meet early or are driven home, you must sign out before you leave.

Warren Dunes

The team will be practicing at Warren Dunes on Sunday, September 16. Meet at MHS. We will drive up to the dunes, have a practice at/near the beach, then do a cookout for lunch. We will return to MHS at 3:30 PM.
