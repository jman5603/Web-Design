Video Embedding Tutorial

Step 1: Open a browser window.

Step 2: Open Youtube or Vimeo.

Step 3: Find the video you wish to embed.

Step 4: Click share.

Step 5: Click embed.

Step 6: Copy the provided code.

Step 7: Paste the code into your HTML file.

Step 8: Save your file.

Step 9: Your video is successfully embedded in your file!
